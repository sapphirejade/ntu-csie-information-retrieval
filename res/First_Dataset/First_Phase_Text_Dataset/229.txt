MICROSCOPIC:  (Reported by Dr E Whitemore)
The gallbladder has been examined in its entirety. Sections of gallbladder show chronic cholecystitis with extensive adenomyosis. There is variably prominent fibromuscular wall thickening, widespread Rokitansky Aschoff sinus formation and patchy mild chronic inflammation. There also shows scattered foci of intestinal metaplasia and regenerative change of the gallbladder epithelium. No evidence of dysplasia or malignancy is seen. 

DIAGNOSIS:
Gallbladder: Chronic cholecystitis, cholelithiasis, focal intestinal metaplasia and extensive adenomyosis. No evidence of dysplasia or invasive malignancy.
